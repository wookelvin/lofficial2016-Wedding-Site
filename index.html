The Timeline

We met briefly during our lunch period in grade 9.

We finally had a class together, Grade 10 English, and that’s where our friendship started to blossom (aka daily late night convos on msn talking about a whole lot of nothing until 3am).

It wasn’t until grade 11 that we started dating officially. Ambrose surprised me after school dressed in a bright green dress shirt, holding flowers, and with a super mushy (sweet) letter. Ambrose has always been the dramatic type. ;)

Throughout high school we only had 3 classes together. 1 of these came after we started dating, which was Grade 12 World Issues. We picked each other for group projects, arrived late together, fell asleep during class together. Inseparable. Mr. Johnston had an unorthodoxed teaching style and liked to keep it real. On the last day of class he gave each student a word of advice. "Jambrose, you need to spread your wings and let each other fly!" As a couple of teenagers joined at the hip, we had a hunch about what he was saying. Maybe a reasonable amount of space to allow each other to grow could benefit our relationship. A few months go by and we begin our undergrad together at the University of Toronto.

Despite how inseparable we were, we faced a lot of challenges in the last decade. Many obstacles, tears, and growing pains later, we finally realized how to be the best person for each other.

Finally, after 11 years, Ambrose proposed to Jacqueline on December 26, 2014 during their annual Christmas party with close friends. The months of stress prior to the proposal (keeping it a secret, living a double life) was worth it because Jacqueline had no idea it was coming and was completely surprised.

WHERE

Our celebration will take place at Palais Royale in Downtown Toronto. The address is 1601 Lake Shore Blvd West, Toronto ON, M6K 3C1. There is parking available directly in front of Palais Royale on Lake Shore Blvd. Please refer to “HOW" for travel directions.

The ceremony will be outside at the Courtyard Patio, followed by cocktails and hor d’oeuvres. Reception dinner and the epic after party will be inside the Ballroom.

HOW

By Flight

For our out of town guests, fly into Toronto Pearson Airport (airport code YYZ) or Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (airport code YTZ).

By TTC

Two options, (1) From Bloor subway line, get off at Dundas West station, take the 504 streetcar which will head south on Roncesvalles, then get off at the intersection of Roncesvalles, King, and Queen and walk south across the footbridge, or (2) From downtown Toronto, take the King streetcar 504 or Queen streetcar 501 westbound, get off at the intersection of Roncesvalles, King, and Queen and walk south across the footbridge.

By Car From The West

Take the Gardiner Expressway East and exit onto Lake Shore Blvd East. Stay in the left-hand lane and look for Green coloured Palais Royale signage. Immediately after the venue, make a left at the stop sign, and follow the turnoff into the parking lot.

By Car from the East

Take the Gardiner Expressway West to the exit at Dunn Ave./Jameson Ave. Stay left, take Lake Shore Blvd West, and stay in the left-hand lane. Palais Royale Parking is on the left side with signage indicating the entrance.
